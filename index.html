New Dawn Deli

Fresh Starts Daily

New Dawn Deli at Food Hall TO is a welcoming, community-focused deli serving fresh soups, sandwiches, and salads crafted from locally sourced ingredients. Our mission is to empower survivors of abuse through trauma-informed culinary training, providing meaningful employment, skill development, and a supportive environment. By combining quality food with social impact, we offer both delicious meals and opportunities for growth and independence.
