New Dawn Deli

Fresh Starts Daily

New Dawn Deli at Food Hall TO is a community-focused deli serving soups, sandwiches, and salads crafted from locally sourced ingredients. Our mission is to empower survivors of abuse through culinary training, providing them with meaningful employment, skill development, and a supportive environment. By combining quality food with social impact, we're serving great meals and an even greater purpose!
